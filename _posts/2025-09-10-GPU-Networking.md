
Yesterday, I came across this nice series of articles about **GPU Networking**, by [Austin Lyons](https://substack.com/@chipstrat).<br>

1. [GPU Networking Basics, Part 1](https://www.chipstrat.com/p/gpu-networking-basics-part-1)<br>
   Communication during Training, Scale Out, Scale Up, Network Architectures, Switches, etc.

2. [GPU Networking Basics, Part 2](https://www.chipstrat.com/p/gpu-networking-basics-part-2)<br>
   Front-End & Back-End, North-South & East-West Traffic, DeepSeek Example

3. [GPU Networking Basics Part 3: Scale-Out](https://www.chipstrat.com/p/gpu-networking-basics-part-3-scale)<br>
